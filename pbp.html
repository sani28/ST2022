Global Brand Values

Brand Values

Much effort went into the final visuals that were to represent the company's new global brand values. From a proposal of 3 different art directions from the deisgn team, my iconography was selected to be used on postcards, swag and other employee materials for the launch of the brand values. I was happy to see the brand try new things and shift from a standard 2d icon style into illustative imagery with human scenes and figures. It was an interesting challenge to depict accurate meanings of the brand words - coming up with scenes, focusing on minute details of the people, their gestures or actions and how people percieved them.
